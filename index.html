About Us

This online book store is created from the love of fictional characters and what they have represented over the the years, with iconic characters like Superman & Spider-Man both from different publications representing Hope in ourselves and others, and anti-heroes like Batman & Daredevil representing the grey morality of society and ourselves. We as Project Kr. grew up on these values and would love to share these values, triumphs, defeates and lives of these heroes in graphic format, for your enjoyment first and foremost. Project Kr. is for the book lovers, by the booklovers.
